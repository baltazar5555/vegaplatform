How KGR works

1
Hypothesis
A proposed change enters the review pipeline

3
Evidence Check
Every claim must be verified against source material
4

This is a simulated demonstration of the KGR protocol structure. In production, each reviewer is a separate AI model with no access to other reviewers' outputs. VEGA KGR has completed 80+ adversarial review rounds across multiple domains.
